Our History

New Engineering Works and Supply Limited (NEWS) is a proud sister company of Earth Construction Company Nigeria Limited (ECC), a reputable indigenous engineering firm registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission in 2007. The roots of NEWS date back to 1995, when it began as a consortium of skilled engineers providing civil engineering services for various clients. With a 10% expatriate quota, NEWS is firmly grounded in its commitment to delivering exceptional engineering solutions while prioritizing local expertise.

The parent company, ECC, initially specialized in subcontracting for renowned multinational companies such as RCC, Strabag Construction Company, and Julius Berger PLC. To further expand its contributions to Nigeriaâ€™s infrastructural development, NEWS was established as an extension of ECC, enabling greater capacity and broader service delivery.

Starting with a modest team of four engineers, two surveyors, and three foremen specializing in earthworks and concrete, NEWS has grown into a dynamic organization boasting over 150 professionals. These include highly qualified engineers, technicians, surveyors, administrative staff, and accountants, both local and expatriate. NEWS leverages extensive expertise to offer a comprehensive range of services, including:

  1. Engineering feasibility studies
  2. Planning and design of structural works
  3. Road construction
  4. Construction supervision and project management
Today, NEWS is recognized for its dedication to excellence and has earned the trust of the Federal Government of Nigeria, key agencies, and several state governments. With a proven track record in delivering impactful civil and infrastructural projects, NEWS continues to contribute significantly to Nigeria's engineering and infrastructural development.
